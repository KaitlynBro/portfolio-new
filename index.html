About Me

I am a front-end web developer, based in Toronto, Ontario, with a passion for creating beautifully designed, clean, and fully-responsive websites.

My favourite thing about web development is that there is always more to learn. The tech industry is forever growing and changing, which makes each day an exciting one. I am looking to find a place in a collaborative, supportive company, where I can contribute my own skills to enhance a company's online platform, as well as continuing to learn everything I can.

When I'm not behind my computer screen, you can find me reading, listening to podcasts, or looking at pictures of cats.
